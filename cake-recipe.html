Overview

  • A soft, buttery cake perfect for all occasions.

Details

  • Prep Time: 20 min
  • Cook Time: 30 min
  • Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • Flour, sugar, eggs, butter, milk, baking powder, vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Beat eggs with butter and sugar.
  3. Combine everything.
  4. Bake, cool, and serve.
