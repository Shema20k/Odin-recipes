Overview

  • Juicy, spiced oven-roasted chicken with a golden crust.

Details

  • Prep Time: 15 min
  • Cook Time: 40 min
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs, olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Season chicken with spices and oil.
  2. Roast in oven until golden and juicy.
  3. Optional: garnish with herbs.
